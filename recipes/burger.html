Cheeseburger

This is a simple recipe for making a cheeseburger, a classic American dish that is always a crowd-pleaser.

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into 4 patties and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until cooked to your liking.
  4. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on each patty to melt.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Assemble the burgers by placing the patties on the buns and adding your favorite toppings.
