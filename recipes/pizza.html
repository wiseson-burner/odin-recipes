Margherita Pizza

This is a simple recipe for making a Margherita pizza, a classic Italian dish that is always a favorite.

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup of tomato sauce
  • 8 ounces of fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Slice the mozzarella cheese and arrange it on top of the sauce.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, and add fresh basil leaves.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
  8. Remove from the oven, let cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
