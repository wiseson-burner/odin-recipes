Pilau Rice

This is a simple recipe for making pilau rice, a flavorful and aromatic dish that pairs well with various main courses.

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a large pot, bring the water to a boil.
  3. Add the rice, salt, and oil to the boiling water.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and simmer for 18-20 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes.
  6. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve.
